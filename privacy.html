Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MetroMelb is a free Melbourne public transport companion app. We take your privacy seriously.

What we collect

  • Anonymous user ID (randomly generated, stored only on your device)
  • Push notification token (to send you alerts)
  • Notification preferences you set in the app
  • Community reports and feed posts you choose to submit (anonymous)
  • Approximate location (only to find your nearest station — never stored)

What we don't collect

  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Precise location history
  • Payment information
  • Any data sold to third parties

Third party services

  • Firebase — stores anonymous reports and feed posts
  • Transport Victoria (PTV) — provides live train/tram/bus data
  • Expo — delivers push notifications

Data retention

Community reports are automatically deleted after 30 minutes. Feed posts are deleted after 24 hours. Push tokens are deleted if unused for 90 days.
